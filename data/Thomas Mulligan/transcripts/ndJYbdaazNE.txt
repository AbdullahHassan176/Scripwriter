One thing that really keeps me up at night is the mystery of what Nicola Tesla was really working on. Near the end of his life, Nicola Tesla became increasingly secretive about his research and inventions. One project in particular was a super weapon that he referred to as the Teleforce. But in the years since has become known as the Death Ray, a machine that he claimed would be used to end all wars by shooting a beam of high energy particles across vast distances. As countries moved closer to the beginning of World War II, Tesla continued to brag about the death ray to any reporter who would listen. and tried to sell his plans to various governments including the US, the UK, and the USSR. When he passed away in 1943 after spending years in isolation, a US government agency called the Office of Alien Property seized all of Tesla's belongings and papers related to any research he was conducting in his hotel room. It is now widely believed that he wasn't anywhere close to the invention he claimed to be on the cusp of.
